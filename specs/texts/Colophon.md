## Design

The website was designed in [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/), guided by the Japanese aesthetic of _shibui_ (渋い)—a restrained, understated beauty rooted in the Muromachi period. Further reading: [the inspiration](https://www.stefanimhoff.de/new-website-2020-inspiration/) and [the design process](https://www.stefanimhoff.de/new-website-2020-design/).

Typography uses the variable typeface [Secuela](https://fontlibrary.org/en/font/secuela-variable) by [deFharo](https://github.com/defharo), scaled on the golden ratio and responsive to viewport width.

Cover images and the portrait were generated with [Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai/), [FLUX](https://blackforestlabs.ai/), [ControlNet](https://github.com/lllyasviel/ControlNet), [GPT Image 1](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-image-1), [Gemini 3 Pro Image](https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-image/pro/), and [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/). Icons are from [Remix Icon](https://remixicon.com/) by [Jimmy Cheung](https://github.com/xiaochunjimmy) and [Wendy Gao](https://github.com/wendygaoyuan).

## Technology

Built with [Astro](https://astro.build/). Written in [Neovim](https://neovim.io/) with [GitHub Copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot), [Claude Code](https://code.claude.com/), and [Raycast AI](https://www.raycast.com/pro). Components in Astro and [React](https://react.dev/). Content in [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/). Styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) and [tailwindcss-logical](https://stevecochrane.github.io/tailwindcss-logical/). Search via [Pagefind](https://pagefind.app/). Self-hosted with [Coolify](https://coolify.io/).

Source code is on [GitHub](https://github.com/kogakure/website-astro-stefanimhoff.de). You are welcome to learn from it. Build your own things.
