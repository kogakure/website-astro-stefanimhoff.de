Designed in [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/), guided by the Japanese aesthetic of _shibui_ (<Ruby base="渋い" />)—a restrained, understated beauty rooted in the Muromachi period. Further reading: [the inspiration](/new-website-2020-inspiration/) and [the design process](/new-website-2020-design/).

Typography uses the variable typefaces [Boska](https://productiontype.com/family/boska) by Production Type and [Switzer](https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/switzer) by Fontshare, scaled on the golden ratio and responsive to viewport width.

Cover images and the portrait were generated with [Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai/), [FLUX](https://blackforestlabs.ai/), [ControlNet](https://github.com/lllyasviel/ControlNet), [GPT Image 1](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-image-1), [Gemini 3 Pro Image](https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-image/pro/), and [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/). Icons are from [Remix Icon](https://remixicon.com/) by [Jimmy Cheung](https://github.com/xiaochunjimmy) and [Wendy Gao](https://github.com/wendygaoyuan).
