Designed in [Affinity](https://www.affinity.studio/), guided by the Japanese aesthetic of _shibui_ (<Ruby base="渋い" />)—a restrained, understated beauty rooted in the Muromachi period. Learn more about the [Design System](/design-system/).

Typography uses the variable typefaces [Boska](https://fontshare.com/fonts/boska) and [Switzer](https://fontshare.com/fonts/switzer), scaling responsive to the viewport width.

Cover images and the portrait were generated with various models, including [GPT Image 1](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-image-1), [GPT Image 2](https://developers.openai.com/api/docs/models/gpt-image-2), and [Gemini 3 Pro Image](https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-image/pro/). Icons are using [Phosphor Icons](https://phosphoricons.com/).
