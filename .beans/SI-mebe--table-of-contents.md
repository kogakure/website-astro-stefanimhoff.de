The blog posts of the "Writing" section should have a table of contents (TOC) showing the second-level headings (H2) of the post. The TOC should be displayed at the top of the post, next to the content in the left two columns. It should be interactive and follow the scroll position of the user and highlight the current section in the TOC. The TOC should also be collapsible on smaller screens for better mobile usability.
